Everything you need to know about our services

What are your salon hours?

+
We are open from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. We recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure your preferred time slot is available.

How do I book an appointment?

+
You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 92299 15277, messaging us on WhatsApp, or visiting our salon directly. We also accept walk-ins based on availability.

What products do you use?

+
We use only premium, professional-grade products from trusted international and Indian brands. Our products are carefully selected for quality, safety, and effectiveness.

Do you offer bridal packages?

+
Yes! We offer comprehensive bridal packages that include pre-bridal treatments, makeup, hair styling, and more. Contact us for a personalized consultation and package details.

What is your cancellation policy?

+
We request at least 2 hours notice for cancellations. This helps us accommodate other clients and maintain our service quality. For bridal appointments, please provide 24 hours notice.

Do you offer services for men?

+
Absolutely! Blancbeu is a family salon that caters to men, women, and children. We offer a full range of grooming services for men including haircuts, beard styling, facials, and more.
