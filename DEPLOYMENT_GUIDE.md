## Your App is Production Ready!

Your premium Beauty Family Salon app is complete and ready to deploy to the world.

## One-Click Deployment to Netlify

### Step 1: Click Publish
- Look for the **"Publish"** button in the top-right corner of Replit
- Click it now

### Step 2: Select Netlify
- Choose **"Netlify"** as your deployment platform
- This will open the Netlify connection screen

### Step 3: Connect Account
- Sign in with your Netlify account (or create a free account)
- Authorize Replit to deploy your app
- Takes less than 1 minute

### Step 4: Deploy
- Your app automatically deploys
- You'll get a live URL in 2-3 minutes
- Example: `https://beauty-family-salon.netlify.app`
